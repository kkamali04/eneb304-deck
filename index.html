Gunshot Detection Circuit

A $3 BJT preamplifier with electret microphone that detects gunshots at 10+ meters. Single-stage common-emitter amplifier using the 2N2222A transistor.

01. The Question

Can a $3 Circuit Detect Gunshots?

Commercial gunshot detection systems like ShotSpotter cost $50,000 to $100,000 per square mile to deploy. They use distributed sensor arrays, GPS synchronization, and cloud-based machine learning.

But the fundamental building block — the analog front end that converts sound to measurable voltage — can be as simple as a microphone and one transistor.

05. Frequency Domain

FFT Reveals the Spectrum

Far: ~2.3 kHz
Low-frequency energy dominates. Atmospheric absorption attenuates highs over 10m distance.
Medium: ~6.9 kHz
Broader spectrum. More high-frequency content preserved at closer range.
Cap-Coupled: ~20 kHz
High-pass filtering shifts energy upward. fL = 1/(2π·4.8k·0.1μF) = 332 Hz.
06. Energy Envelope

How Energy Decays

Hilbert envelope tracks instantaneous amplitude. Medium distance shows most abrupt onset. Capacitor-coupled energy lingers due to RC time constant.

08. Theory

Why This Works

# Common-Emitter Amplifier
Voltage Gain:
Av = -gm * RC = -(IC/VT) * RC
Av = -(1mA/26mV) * 10k = -385

Input Impedance:
Zin = (β+1) * re = 101 * 26Ω
Zin = 2.6 kΩ ≈ Zmic = 2.2 kΩ ✓

Bandwidth:
fL = 332 Hz, fH = 777 kHz
Covers 2-20 kHz gunshot spectrum
~385x Voltage Gain
5 mV mic signal → ~1.9 V output. Well above noise floor and easily detectable by a comparator.
99.3% Power Transfer
Near-perfect impedance match between microphone output (2.2k) and amplifier input (2.6k).
~40 dB Gain Budget
Enough to amplify sub-millivolt acoustic signals to the 1-2V range for reliable detection.

13. Conclusions

What We Learned

1. ✓ Detection at 10m+
Single-stage CE amplifier produces 1.2-1.9V peaks from real gunshot events.
2. ✓ ~400x Gain
Measured gain consistent with theory (Av = -385). ~40 dB gain budget.
3. ✓ 2-20 kHz Content
Frequency content well within amplifier's 332 Hz - 777 kHz bandwidth.
4. ✓ Impedance Match
2.6k input matches 2.2k mic output. 99.3% power transfer efficiency.
